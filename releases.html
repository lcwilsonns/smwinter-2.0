About Us

Welcome to Our Stitch Skin Dermatology & Aesthetics

At Stitch Skin, Our team of experienced wedding photographers is dedicated to making your dreams come true. Whether you're looking to renovate your home, create a stylish office environment, or design a retail space, we've got you covered. From concept to completion, we'll work closely with you to ensure every detail aligns with your vision.

We're dermatologist and aesthetics experts with hearts on our sleeves, who believe that every person deserves to be immortalised in breathtaking beauty.

Finding your dream wedding photographer shouldn't be stressful! Here's how to make it magical:

  1. Determine your reason for seeking help: Fill out our Contact form with your vision, dreams, and what makes your love unique. The more we know, the better we capture your essence.
  2. Research dermatologists in your area: Schedule a free consultation to get to know us, ask questions, and see if we're the perfect fit. We'll share packages, show our work, and make sure you feel confident you've found your photographer soulmates.
  3. Schedule an appointment: We offer flexible packages for every love story and budget. Choose from engagement sessions, full wedding coverage, or even destination adventures!
  4. Prepare for your first appointment: Once you've found your perfect package and feel the connection, sign the contract and secure your date. The excitement begins!
  5. Attend your appointment: We'll help you create timelines, answer questions, and ensure you feel relaxed and ready to radiate joy on your special day.
  6. Follow your treatment plan: After the confetti settles, you'll receive beautifully edited photos to relive every moment for years to come.
