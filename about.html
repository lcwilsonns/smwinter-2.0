about the author

meet s.m. winter

S. M. Winter lives in slightly rural Ontario where she spends her days working full-time and hanging out with her husband, daughter, and pets. During quiet evenings she hides out in bed with her computer and airpods and moonlights as a poet, unleashing her inner emo. Grade 8 her would be so proud of where she's at.

Her first self-published collection Poems of Divorce was born while she navigated a divorce through the second half of her twenties. Originally these poems were an outlet of therapy never intended to leave her iPhone's notes app. However, she decided to take a terrifying leap and do the damn thing.

She's currently on do not disturb working on her next collection - Poems of Lost. Stay tuned!
